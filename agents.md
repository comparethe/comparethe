# Agent Notes

## Lifecycle Email Platforms

Primary category for this comparison:

- `Lifecycle email / customer engagement platforms`: tools built for welcome emails, onboarding sequences, follow-ups, event-triggered messaging, and light broadcast email.

Adjacent categories to keep separate:

- `Transactional email APIs`: SES, Postmark, Resend, Mailgun.
- `Newsletter / creator email platforms`: Beehiiv, Substack, Mailchimp, Kit.
- `CRM / marketing suites`: HubSpot, ActiveCampaign.

Current focus lives in [`Lifecycle Email Platforms/README.md`](./Lifecycle%20Email%20Platforms/README.md).

## Short Description

Lifecycle email platforms sit between raw delivery infrastructure and newsletter-first tools. They connect signup events, user behavior, segments, and scheduled automations so teams can send welcome emails, follow-up sequences, and event-driven messages without building everything on top of a low-level email API.

Examples: Loops, Customer.io, Userlist, Kit, Brevo.
