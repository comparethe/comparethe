# Userlist

![Userlist homepage screenshot](https://screenshotit.app/userlist.com)

## Snapshot

- `Best for`: SaaS companies that want lifecycle email without a giant marketing suite.
- `Positioning`: Purpose-built lifecycle email and messaging for SaaS.
- `Why it stands out`: Focused product and clear SaaS orientation.

## Assessment

Userlist is unusually opinionated around the exact use case this page targets. It is not trying to be a broad all-in-one marketing cloud. It is closer to a SaaS-native lifecycle tool with a simpler mental model than Customer.io.

That focus is appealing, but the price point is harder to justify for very small teams. It looks strongest for companies that specifically want lifecycle email for SaaS users and care less about broad newsletter or creator tooling.

## Trade-offs

- Strong category fit for product onboarding and lifecycle messaging.
- Narrower and more focused than Brevo or Mailchimp.
- Simpler than Customer.io, but also less extensible.
- Entry price is high relative to Loops or Kit.

## Pricing

- `Free tier`: No.
- `Typical paid starting point`: around `$149/mo`.
- `Interpretation`: best for teams that already know lifecycle email is important enough to pay for a dedicated tool.

## Verdict

Userlist is a credible specialist choice, but the pricing makes it harder to recommend as the default first tool.
